Designskolen Kolding 2012 \\ Service Design Course \\ October 2012


The Concept

Connecting human and nature, putting humans in a natural context


The Challenge

How might we create a family experience that enables learning and playing at the Kongeaaen?

Meet the Hansens


A Mirrorhouse!

Partly camouflaged, partly obvious, the mirror cube allows reflection on the relationship between humanity and nature, and to see oneself within this context

A Touchscreen!


Entering the room, the screen lights up


The round screen allows information browsing
while maintaining social interaction


The narrative is designed so that past, present, nature and civilization
are brought together into one coherent 'storyline'

Mini-maps!


The map follows the walking path and points out areas of interest


The icons on the map apply to the information on the touchscreen


It's easy to fold and small enough to fit any pocket


When put together, the three maps, one for each part of the path, complete the illustration on the back
